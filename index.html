Why it works

Built for people who've tried and quit before.

Five minutes counts
Breathwork works in small doses. Even a single two-minute session activates your nervous system's calm response. MindPace treats every session — however short — as real progress.
Progress, not a streak
Streaks punish you for missing a day. The MindPace contribution graph just shows your cumulative dose — so a week with two good sessions looks like what it is: a week with two good sessions.
Your pace, literally
The breath pacer guides your inhale and exhale at whatever rhythm works for you. No guided audio, no instructor — just a rising bar that rises when you breathe in, and falls when you breathe out.
Your dose over time

See your practice the way you see your fitness.

Like a fitness tracker for your nervous system — the contribution graph turns invisible effort into something you can actually look at. Miss a few days? The graph doesn't shame you. It just waits for you to show up again.
